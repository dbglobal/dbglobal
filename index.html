FAQ

Clear answers to common questions.

Does this replace my team?

No. It handles intake and common questions, then routes or escalates when needed.

Can we start after-hours only?

Yes. That’s often the cleanest pilot before expanding coverage.

How do we keep the assistant updated?

Use the Client Updates page to submit approved changes (hours, services, FAQs). We implement updates to keep everything stable.

Do customers need an app?

No. Customers just call the business as usual. The assistant answers through the configured phone workflow.
